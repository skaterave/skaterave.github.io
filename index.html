SkateRave is an color changing LED lighting board designed for roller skates, but can be used on so much more! These are built by hand on a custom printed circuit board. They are designed to be bright and the 8 bit microprocessor allows for satisfying light animations.

  • Modes: 36 unique lighting modes, including solid colors and animations, that are toggled with the push of a button
  • Battery Life: Runs on a 9v battery with 10+ hours of continuous battery life (depending on the mode)
  • On/Off Swtich: No need to connect or disconnect the battery to control power; there is an onboard switch.
  • Mode Memory: Once you have selected your favorite mode, it will be remembered when turning it on and off. More time skating, less time fiddling.
  • Hackable: For the nerds in the room, this runs an Atmega328P-AU and has an exposed ICSP interface. Meaning, it's totally customizable with the Arduino family of tools if one was so inclined!
